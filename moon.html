Moons

Jupiter's Moon

A basic sub-division is a grouping of the eight inner regular moons, which have nearly circular orbits near the plane of Jupiter's equator and are believed to have formed with Jupiter.

Io

This is one of the four moons that orbit around Jupiter. The are called The Galilean moons. In order of increasing distance from Jupiter: Io, Europa, Ganymede, Callisto are the four moons of Jupiter.
